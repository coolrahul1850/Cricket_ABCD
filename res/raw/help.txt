Welcome to Cricket ABCD.

General Instruction
You will start with batting. You can bat 1,3 or 5 overs. You choose a stroke from A to G, A being 1 and G being 6.

Example how it works
You bat A, bowler bows G, Score = 6
You bat G, bowler bows A, Score = 6
You bat B, bowler bows A or C, score = 1
You bat C, bowler bows C , you are out

There are two modes in this game, regular and hong kong sixes. Hong kong sixes you can bowl and bat only A or G.
